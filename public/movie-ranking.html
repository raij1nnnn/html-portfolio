The Best Movies According to Sasha

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Interstellar

One of the best Christofer Nolan movies, beautiful pictures, an amazing and fitting score, touching story, and quite accurate from a scientific point of view.

Your Name

A contender for one of the best animated movies with the likes of Spirited Away. A very heartwarming and unusual romance that you can't help but fall in love with.

Harry Potter Saga

Not a single movie but still deserves to be here. If you loved it as a kid, chances are you still love it and you won't stop loving it. No need for explanation on this one.
